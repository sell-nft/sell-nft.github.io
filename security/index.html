NFT Smart Contract Security & Asset Custody

Evaluating contract verification, custody models, and safety safeguards.

Audited Trading Contracts

Trading contracts undergo thorough audits to ensure they are free of exploits, utilizing secure libraries like the OpenZeppelin contracts framework.

Verifying Metadata Integrity

To prevent phishing, the platform screens collections to verify their official smart contract addresses, conforming to high-grade security systems defined in the NIST digital security guidelines.

Non-Custodial Listings

When listing an NFT for sale, the item remains inside your own wallet until a trade is executed. You do not lock your collectibles inside a marketplace vault, keeping your assets secure.
